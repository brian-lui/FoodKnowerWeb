About FoodKnower

Our Mission

FoodKnower exists to help people see through the noise of modern food marketing. Too many foods that look "healthy" on the shelf are actually ultra-processed, engineered products designed to make you eat more than you need.

We believe everyone deserves a simple, honest answer to the question: "Is this food actually good for me?"

What We Do

FoodKnower uses AI to identify any food from a photo and scores it on two things that matter most for healthy eating:

  • Food Integrity — how far the food has been industrially processed, based on the NOVA classification system and our own extended analysis.
  • Overeating Risk — how engineered the food is to override your natural feelings of fullness, based on peer-reviewed hyperpalatability research.

No calorie counting. No complicated meal plans. Just point, snap, and know.

About FoodKnower

Our Mission

We want to bring you a more effective way to eat for health and weight control. Too many foods look "healthy", but are ultra-processed foods that make you overeat. And there are many foods where no one can tell if they're good or bad for you: "vegetable-based" snacks? Air-baked potato chips? Pea crisps?

We believe everyone deserves a simple, honest answer to the question: "Is this food actually good for me?"

What We Do

FoodKnower uses AI to identify any food from a photo and scores it on two things that matter most for healthy eating:

  • Food Integrity — how much the food has been industrially processed.
  • Portion Control — how much the food will override your natural feelings of fullness.

Most people try calorie counting or make complicated meal plans. But FoodKnower is simpler. We believe that if your food makes you feel full naturally, you'll eat the right amount. And if your food is healthy, you'll sustain this way of eating.

Just point, snap, and know.

From the Founder

I created this app after finding out that it didn't exist. Something that would let me know what foods to prefer and what foods to avoid. Something that trusted me to make good choices, as long as I had the information I needed.

If you're here, I believe that you also deserve this trust. I hope FoodKnower lets you do what you've always wanted.
